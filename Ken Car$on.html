About Ken Car$on

Full Name: Kenyatta Frazier Jr.

Born: April 11, 2000

Hometown: Atlanta, Georgia

Ken Car$on, signed to Playboi Carti’s Opium label, quickly rose to fame with his unique mix of trap, punk, and “rage” style rap. Known for his high-energy performances and gritty sound, he’s captivated a new generation of rap fans.
